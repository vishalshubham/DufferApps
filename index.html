UW Info Session

Info sessions right in your hand

UWIS (University of Waterloo Information Session) helps students to provide simple, easy and efficient access to all the Employer information sessions happening at the University of Waterloo. App provides info session specific details as well for current, previous and next term. App is integrated with Google maps for easy navigation to the location where specific info session is happening.

You can download this app from play store for free, click here: UW Info Session.

  • Date: June 2017
  • Android Version: 4.1+ (Jelly Bean and up)
  • Category: Events
